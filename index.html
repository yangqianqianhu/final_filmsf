About this map

If you love movies, and you love San Francisco, you're bound to love this -- a map of the listing of filming locations of movies shot in San Francisco starting from 1924. You'll find the titles, locations, fun facts, names of the director, writer, actors, and studio for most of these films.

Data Provided by San Francisco Film Commission
